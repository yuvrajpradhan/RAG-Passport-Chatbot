## Documents Required for a Fresh Passport
Applicants are advised to visit their jurisdictional Passport office's home page to know about any additional documents required. Applicants are required to furnish original documents along with one set of self-attested photocopies at the Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) for processing.

## Special Cases: Marriage, Divorce, and Separation
For addition of Spouse Name: Marriage Certificate OR a Joint Photo Declaration signed by both husband and wife (Annexure J).

For deletion of Spouse Name: Divorce Order/Decree.

For Change of Spouse Name: Divorce Order/Decree OR Death Certificate of the first spouse, AND a Re-marriage certificate OR a Joint Photo Declaration signed by both husband and wife (Annexure J).

For change of surname by women after marriage/divorce: Marriage Certificate OR Joint Photo Declaration OR a Divorce Order/Decree. In case of a full change of name after marriage, the standard name change procedure should be followed.

## Rules for Minor Applicants
It is assumed that the consent of both parents is available unless specified otherwise.

For minor applicants, the present address proof document can be in the name of the parent(s).

It is advised to carry original and self-attested copies of the parents' Passports to the Passport Seva Kendra (PSK), if they possess them.

For minor applicants, documents can be attested by parents.

The minor applicant is eligible for Non-ECR status until he/she attains the age of 18 years.

## ECR / ECNR Status
ECR stands for Emigration Check Required and ECNR stands for Emigration Check Not Required.

ECR status will be printed in the Passport of applicants who fall in the ECR category. For those in the Non-ECR category, there will be no specific mention in the Passport.

The practice of putting a Non-ECR (previously ECNR) stamp has been discontinued.

## Application Fees and Payments
Police Clearance Certificate (PCC): The fee for a PCC is Rs. 500.

Mandatory Prior Payment: Prior payment of the applicable Passport service fee is mandatory for booking appointments at all Passport Seva Kendras.

Online Payment Process: After submitting the application, you must start the payment and scheduling process. Select the Online Payment option.

## Scheduling an Appointment
Booking an Appointment: Select the ‘Appointment Quota’ (Normal/Tatkaal). Tatkaal is only available for Tatkaal applications. Select the preferred Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK). The system will display the next available appointment date. After verifying the details, click the ‘Pay and Book Appointment’ button to be redirected to the payment gateway.

Appointment Confirmation: After successful payment, you will be redirected to the Passport Seva Online Portal, where the Appointment Confirmation screen will be displayed with your appointment details.

Rescheduling: Users can reschedule appointments three times, except in the case of a Tatkaal application, which is restricted to only one reschedule.

Visiting the Office: Bring a printed copy of the online-filled application form and your original documents when visiting the Consular, Passport and Visa (CPV) Division or the Passport Office (PO) for application submission.
